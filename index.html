The opportunity gate

It can tell you not to build.

Most AI business systems are trained to finish. WORKCELL ZERO is trained to check first. A bad idea stopped early is a successful run.

Not every idea should become a business. Before anything gets built, WORKCELL ZERO makes one of four calls:

Build
Evidence supports a first version
Narrow
A smaller, sharper opening
Use Existing
Something already solves it
Stop
The evidence says do not build
Every decision comes with receipts.

Honest on purpose

Built to challenge, not flatter.

The line is useful even when the answer is no. That is not a weakness in the offer. It is the reason you can trust a yes.

What it does

  • Researches the opening and keeps the sources behind every material claim
  • Builds one complete working customer outcome, not a report or a mockup
  • Prepares the selling path and one cheap test aimed at the most dangerous assumption

What it will not promise

  • Guaranteed sales, profit, or a market that wants your idea
  • Automatic publishing, advertising, or spending on your behalf
  • A validated business without a real test in the field
