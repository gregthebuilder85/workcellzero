The difference

Trained to stop.

Most AI business systems are trained to finish. WORKCELL ZERO is trained to check first, and you can inspect the receipts behind every call.

Before anything gets built, the opportunity gate makes one of four calls. A bad idea stopped early is a successful run.

See what happens inside each workcell

SPARK. Strip away the attached solution. Capture the customer, the repeated problem, the assets on hand, the limits, and the unknowns, then restate the idea as one neutral research question.

SCAN. Research demand, existing behavior, spending, alternatives, reachability, constraints, and the evidence against the idea. Every material claim is logged in an Evidence Ledger with its source.

LOCK. Return Build, Narrow, Use Existing, or Stop at the opportunity gate. Preserve the claim-level receipts and name the dangerous unknown.

BLUEPRINT. Compare three different business shapes. Select the smallest credible offer and the first field test.

BUILD. Build one complete customer outcome with starter data, a quit rule, and tests. The workcell never labels an untested artifact production-ready.

LAUNCH. Create the offer, the selling page, a price hypothesis, the payment or lead path, and the Final Inspection.

TEST. Choose the cheapest experiment capable of challenging the most dangerous assumption.

DECIDE. Read behavior, not applause. Continue, change, narrow, or stop, with the previous version preserved.

Type SHOW RECEIPTS at any point to see exactly why the cell believes what it believes. When live research is unavailable, WORKCELL ZERO says so. It does not present sample material as market evidence.

Built for long sessions

Reliability and recovery.

Long AI sessions can drift, and platforms change under every product built on them. SYSTEM 01 checks its environment before starting, re-anchors at every stage, and can save or restore the complete project state in a fresh conversation.

On every run

  • A boot check tests web research and file creation before the run begins, and reports exactly what your account can do
  • A stage status line and re-anchoring at every stage boundary keep the OS on protocol
  • When live research is unavailable, the workcell says so. It never presents sample material as market evidence

When something slips

  • RECALIBRATE re-reads the rules mid-run
  • SAVE STATE prints a portable recovery block
  • LOAD STATE rebuilds the project in a new chat, on either platform
  • SHOW RECEIPTS opens the evidence behind the current decision
  • The common tested failure modes are documented in the kit with recovery steps

Questions

Read this first.

Do I need to know prompt engineering?
No prompts to write. You install the OS once and answer plain questions in ordinary words.
What do I need for it to run at full power?
Full mode requires access to current web research and file or artifact creation. The boot check tests your account before the run begins and tells you exactly what is available. When either capability is missing, SYSTEM 01 enters reduced mode rather than inventing results.
How long does a complete run take?
The first session is designed to reach a defensible decision and begin the working version. Time varies with the market, the product, and the capabilities available in your AI workspace. The TEST stage runs in the real world over days, because real behavior takes days. A measured range will be published once real run data exists.
What exactly does a working first version mean?
A complete single journey a real customer could use today: real inputs, real logic, a real result, and a next step. It is a working first version you can put in front of a real customer, not production infrastructure. The Final Inspection shows what works, what is simulated, and what still needs to happen before launch.
Does it publish my website automatically?
No. It assembles the publish-ready package: page copy, site files or artifact, offer, price hypothesis, payment or lead instructions, and a go-live checklist. Connecting your domain, checkout account, and taxes remains yours, with exact steps provided.
What happens if research is unavailable?
It tells you, and offers honest options: enable web research, supply your own sources, run direct customer discovery, or pause. It never fills the gap with invented evidence.
What is the difference between this and a custom GPT?
A custom GPT is a container. SYSTEM 01 is what runs inside one: an eight-stage process with evidence rules, decision gates, build standards, an inspection protocol, and recovery commands. It installs into a ChatGPT Project, a custom GPT, or a Claude Project.
Can I move a project between ChatGPT and Claude?
You can move the project state. SAVE STATE prints a structured, versioned project block, and LOAD STATE validates and restores it in a fresh ChatGPT or Claude conversation. Platform-specific artifacts, files, or canvases may need to be reopened or rebuilt in the destination workspace.
Who owns what I build?
You do. Your finished businesses, products, websites, copy, research, and plans are yours to use commercially. The kit files themselves stay under your license and may not be resold.
Does it store my business information?
WORKCELL ZERO does not operate its own project-data server. The kit ships as files you install into your own AI account, and your project is processed and stored according to the settings of the AI platform you use. Information reaches us only when you contact support or deliberately submit material for review.
What support is included?
Installation support by email, covering setup, recovery, missing files, and documented operating failures. It does not include custom product development or private business consulting. The support address and response window are published with the release.
What if my idea is bad?
Then you find out during the first session, with receipts, instead of six months from now. The cell treats a clean stop as a successful outcome, and so should you.
