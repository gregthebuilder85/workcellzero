How it works

Three moves, one line.

You do not learn a system. You answer plain questions and it runs the method, checking its own work and stopping the line when something is wrong.

See the full eight-stage method

SPARK. Strip away the attached solution and restate the idea as one neutral research question.

SCAN. Research demand, spending, alternatives, constraints, and the evidence against the idea. Every material claim is logged with its source.

LOCK. The opportunity gate returns Build, Narrow, Use Existing, or Stop, and names the dangerous unknown.

BLUEPRINT. Compare three business shapes and select the smallest credible one.

BUILD. Build one complete customer outcome with tests. It never labels an untested artifact production-ready.

LAUNCH. Assemble the offer, the selling page, a price hypothesis, and the payment or lead path.

TEST. Run the cheapest experiment that can challenge the most dangerous assumption.

DECIDE. Read behavior, not applause. Continue, change, narrow, or stop, with the previous version preserved.

Long sessions can drift, so the kit checks its environment, re-anchors at every stage, and can save or restore your whole project in a fresh chat. Type SHOW RECEIPTS to see the evidence behind any decision. When live research is unavailable, it says so rather than inventing results.

Before you buy

Straight answers.

What exactly is this?
A kit you install once into a ChatGPT Project, a custom GPT, or a Claude Project. It runs an eight-stage method with evidence rules, decision gates, build standards, and recovery commands. You answer plain questions in ordinary words. No prompts to write.
Do I need anything special to run it?
Full mode uses current web research and file or artifact creation. A boot check tests your account first and tells you what is available. When something is missing, it enters a reduced mode rather than inventing results.
What does a working first version really mean?
A complete single journey a real customer could use today: real inputs, real logic, a real result, and a next step. It is a working first version you can put in front of a real customer, not production infrastructure. The final check shows what works, what is simulated, and what is left to do.
Does it publish my website and run ads for me?
No. It assembles the publish-ready package: page copy, files or artifact, offer, price hypothesis, payment or lead steps, and a go-live checklist. Connecting your domain, checkout, and taxes stays with you, with exact steps provided.
Can I move a project between ChatGPT and Claude?
You can move the project state. It prints a structured, versioned block that restores in a fresh ChatGPT or Claude conversation. Platform-specific artifacts, files, or canvases may need to be reopened or rebuilt in the destination workspace.
What if my idea is bad?
Then you find out during the first session, with receipts, instead of six months from now. A clean stop is a successful outcome, and the 30-day guarantee still applies.
