How your project data is handled

WORKCELL ZERO does not operate its own project-data server. The kit ships as files you install into your own AI account. Your project is processed and stored according to the settings of the AI platform you use, such as ChatGPT or Claude. Information reaches us only when you contact support or deliberately submit material for review.
